Onboarding of AD Service Account has been completed successfully, and you will now want to activate the same in T-Vault and assign permissions to other users who you want to view or reset password.
To do so please complete the following steps:

Activate AD Service Account:

  1. Login to T-Vault.
  2. Navigate to the "Service Accounts" tab - "AD Service Accounts"
  3. Select the AD Service Account you want to activate.
  4. To update any information you need to click on edit button and update the details.
  5. To activate the AD Service Account click on edit button and "Activate Service Account".

Assigning Permissions:

Once you are finished activating your AD Service Account you can assign permissions to other users or groups you want to have permission to view or reset the password for the account.
  1. Login to T-Vault.
  2. Navigate to the "Service Accounts" tab - "AD Service Accounts".
  3. Select the AD Service Account you want to manage.
  4. Select the "Permissions" button.
  5. Navigate to the Users tab and click on "Add User".
  6. Provide the group or user name you want to give permissions to and select either read, reset or deny depending on the level of access you want to give.
Please contact Cloud Support if you need assistance.
